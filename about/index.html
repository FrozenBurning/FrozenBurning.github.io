About me

I’m Zhaoxi Chen, a senior undergraduate student from Tsinghua University, China, and will obtain my bachelor degree in Automation in 2021.

My research interests include deep reinforcement learning, decision-making algorithms for collaborative robotic systems, and interpretation of deep neural networks.
